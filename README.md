# SECURE SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE 2

## Objective

Design a complete layered network architecture that satisfies all five rules for Meridian Power Grid Authority.
- Rule 1: The general public can reach the web server, the outage reporting API, and the billing portal, but must never have any path, direct or indirect, to any internal system.
- Rule 2: Office staff can use internal business tools, but must never be able to reach the SCADA systems under any circumstance, not even accidentally.
- Rule 3: Remote workers and contractors must be verified on every connection, covering identity, device health, and location, before being granted the minimum access their specific role requires and nothing more.
- Rule 4: The SCADA and industrial control systems must be the most isolated zone in the entire network. No traffic enters or leaves that zone except through explicit, approved, tightly controlled pathways. A ransomware infection anywhere else in the organisation must be physically incapable of reaching the substation controllers.
- Rule 5: The security team must have full log visibility across every zone, covering office, SCADA, DMZ, and cloud services, collected in one place in real time, so they can detect and respond to an intrusion within minutes rather than weeks.
